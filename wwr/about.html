Sivar Rafting

Our mission is to provide the safest and most thrilling rafting experience in El Salvador, connecting people with nature.

History

Founded in 2024, Sivar Rafting started as a passion project by a group of friends who loved exploring the Lempa River. We realized that El Salvador offers world-class rapids that few people knew about. What began with a single raft and a dream has grown into the country's premier adventure company, dedicated to showing the world the hidden natural beauty of our land
